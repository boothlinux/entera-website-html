Our wonderful team

They really care about our company

Brad Johnson

Brad Johnson has been in the utility construction business for over 25 years and has been the Operations Manager with Entera since its inception in 2006. Prior to this, he spent over 20 years of his career with one of Entera’s parent companies, Robert B. Somerville. Throughout his career he has held various positions of increasing responsibility and has extensive experience in all aspects of civil utility construction.

Marco Altomare

Marco Altomare joined Entera from Black & McDonald in April 2006 and was appointed Contracts Manager. He is responsible for the overall operations of Entera’s Electrical and Design groups, as well as the management of Entera’s facility located in the east end of Toronto. He has extensive experience with over 25 years in the Utility industry in various roles including Estimating, Project Management, Procurement and Asset Management.
